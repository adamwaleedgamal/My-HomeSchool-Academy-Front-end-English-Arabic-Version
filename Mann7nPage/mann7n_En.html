About Us

At MY HOMESCHOOL ACADEMY, we believe that education is the key to a bright future for every child. We are a leading educational institution that aims to provide an exceptional learning environment combining online interaction with in-person quality, focusing on skill development and talent discovery.

We offer daily follow-ups, periodic assessments to ensure student progress, and psychological support to discover and nurture their talents. Our teaching staff is highly qualified and experienced, committed to providing an environment that fosters creativity and innovation.
